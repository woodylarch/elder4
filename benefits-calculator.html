Get Professional Benefits Advice

As a carer for an elderly person in the UK, you may be entitled to various benefits and financial support. Understanding what benefits you or the person you care for can claim can be complex. That's why we recommend using Turn2Us - the UK's leading benefits calculator and support charity.

  • ✓ Always up-to-date with the latest benefits rules and entitlements
  • ✓ Comprehensive and covers all available benefits including Carer's Allowance, Attendance Allowance, and Pension Credit
  • ✓ Free to use and completely confidential with no registration required
  • ✓ Provides detailed explanations of your entitlements and next steps to claim
  • ✓ Includes information on additional grants and support available to carers and elderly people
